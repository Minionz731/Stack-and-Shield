The Challenge

An established e-commerce platform was losing thousands in revenue every month due to frequent site crashes during peak sales periods. Their infrastructure couldn't handle traffic spikes, and their security posture was weak — making them vulnerable to attacks and data breaches.

Our Solution

We rebuilt their entire infrastructure from the ground up with scalability and security as core priorities. This included cloud-based load balancing, database optimization, full security hardening, and 24/7 monitoring with automated failover systems.

The Results

Since launch, the platform has maintained 99.9% uptime even during their busiest sales events. Load times improved by 40%, customer complaints dropped to near zero, and they've experienced zero security incidents over 12 months of operation.

The Challenge

A professional services firm was drowning in manual lead qualification, client intake, and administrative tasks. Their team spent 60% of their time on repetitive work instead of billable client services, and leads often went cold due to slow response times.

Our Solution
