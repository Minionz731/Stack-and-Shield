FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Everything you need to know before starting your project.

How long does a typical project take?

Most websites launch within 3–6 weeks. Larger web applications typically take 8–12 weeks. You'll get a clear timeline in your proposal — and we hit our dates.

What makes you different from a normal web agency?

Security is built in from the first line of code, not bolted on at the end. Every project ships hardened, monitored, and backed by a real incident-response plan — the same platform, protected for life.

Do you work with existing websites, or only new builds?

Both. We can audit, harden, and optimize your current platform, or rebuild it properly from the ground up. The free security audit tells you exactly what you need.

What happens after launch?

We don't disappear. Our maintenance plans cover 24/7 monitoring, security patches, backups, and performance optimization — with monthly reports so you always know your platform's health.

How much does it cost?

We offer transparent, fixed-price packages plus custom quotes for larger builds. Check our pricing page — no hidden fees, no surprises.
